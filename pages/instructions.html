This is Glitch Witch, an interactive graphic novel. Scroll down through the pages to navigate (using the new-age mac mouse? Drag your fingers up on the mouse to scroll down a page.) Be on the look out for animated hover-able pictures. And be sure to click the play the audio next to each speach bubble to hear the characters talk.

Click Bellatrix's witch's hat to go back to the home page

Click forward arrows to see the next page (some lead back to where you started. when this happens, continue to scroll down, there is more on this page to see)

Want to go back a page? Click the back arrows. No shame in that.
